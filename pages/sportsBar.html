About Us

Welcome to Canary Wharf Sports Bar and Grill, located in the vibrant heart of Canary Wharf, London. Our sports bar and grill is the ultimate destination for sports enthusiasts and food lovers alike. Immerse yourself in the electrifying atmosphere as you cheer on your favorite teams on multiple large screens, ensuring you never miss a moment of the action. Indulge in our mouthwatering menu featuring a wide range of delectable dishes, from juicy burgers and sizzling steaks to flavorful wings and refreshing salads. Whether you're looking to catch up with friends over a few drinks, enjoy a delicious meal, or simply soak up the buzzing energy, Canary Wharf Sports Bar and Grill offers an unforgettable experience. Join us for a memorable sports viewing experience and discover why we are the go-to spot for sports lovers in Canary Wharf.
